Frequently
Asked.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. Ghost Agency is designed to be deployed via simple command-line prompts. If you can type `python deploy.py`, you can run this agency. We provide comprehensive, step-by-step video documentation.

How do I bill my clients?

You own the relationship. You bill your clients directly using Stripe, Razorpay, or standard invoicing software. Ghost Agency does not take a percentage of your retainers. You keep 100% of the revenue.

What if the AI hallucinates or lies?

Our agents utilize strict Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). They are cryptographically locked to the knowledge base you provide for the client. If the answer isn't in the documentation, the AI is programmed to escalate to a human rather than guess.

Are there ongoing platform fees?

Ghost Agency is a one-time purchase. Your only ongoing costs are the API requests made to NVIDIA NIM or OpenAI (which average pennies per day). Alternatively, you can run the models entirely for free locally using the included Ollama integration.
